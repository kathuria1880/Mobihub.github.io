Oppo Find X2

The Find X2 and Find X2 Pro are constructed using an anodized aluminum frame and curved Gorilla Glass 6 on the front. The volume buttons are located on the left side opposite the power button, which has a green accent. Oppo offers two choices of material for the back panel on both phones: the Find X2 is available with either glass or ceramic, and the Find X2 is available with either ceramic or artificial leather.

Oppo F15 Pro

he OPPO R15 Pro has a 6.28-inch AMOLED capacitive display, Octa-core (4x2.2 GHz Kryo 260 & 4x1.8 GHz Kryo 260) Qualcomm Snapdragon 660 processor, 6 GB of RAM and 128 GB of internal storage that can be expanded using microSD cards up to 256 GB. Oppo Reno 3 Pro

Oppo Reno is a line of Android smartphones manufactured by Oppo. Its flagships Oppo Reno, Reno 10x Zoom, and Reno 5G were officially announced on April 10, 2019, while the midrange Reno Z was on May 30, 2019.The Reno and the Reno 10x Zoom both utilize a metal/glass construction, with Gorilla Glass 6 on the front and Gorilla Glass 5 on the back.
